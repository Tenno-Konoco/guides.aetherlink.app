# Glossary

import { Callout, Tab, Tabs } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

There hundreds of `terms/jargon` in the world of **Final Fantasy**. Some of them are very common and used by everyone, while others are very specific to a particular technology or framework. In this article, we will be looking at some of the most common terms used in and out of final fantasy.

<Callout>**Heads up!** some terms may be incorrect or missing.</Callout>

## A

### Apple Sauce
Form of magic that is used to create a variety of different effects. It is a very common form of magic in the world of Final Fantasy.
